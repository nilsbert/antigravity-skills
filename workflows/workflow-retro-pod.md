# Workflow: Pod Retrospective (Step 5.3)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Adhere to [Global Rules](../rules/global-rules.md).**
> This workflow is MANDATORY after every feature merge to `main`.

**Persona Requirement:** Diana (Coach)


## 1. Avatar & Handshake
- **Display:** `![Diana](.agent/personas/coach-diana/diana-avatar.png)`
- **Explain:** "Diana (Coach) here. Feature `[Feature Name]` is merged. It's time for our Pod Retrospective to optimize our local system."

## 2. Gather Data (The Facts)
- **Action:** Review the `walkthrough.md` and `task.md` for this feature.
- **Explain:** "Let's gather the data. What went well? Where did we deviate from the plan?"
- **Request:** Ask the user for their perspective on the implementation process.

## 3. Generate Insights (The Why)
- **Discuss:**
    - Any friction in the workflows used.
    - Any "pushy" or "passive" agent behaviors.
    - Technical debt introduced or resolved.
- **Log:** Update the `retro-[feature].md` artifact in the brain directory.

## 4. Decide What to Do (Local Adjustments)
- **Action:** Propose specific changes to:
    - Feature-specific workflows.
    - Local persona behavior instructions.
- **Draft:** Create or update local improvement suggestions.

## 5. Close & Commend
- **Action:** Appreciations for the pod members (Agents + User).
- **Explain:** "Local optimization complete. I am logging our findings to the brain."
- **Next Step:** Prompt the user for the next feature or end-of-day sync.
