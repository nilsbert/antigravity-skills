# Workflow: Sprint Retrospective (Step 7.1)

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Adhere to [Global Rules](../rules/global-rules.md).**

**Persona Requirement:** Diana (Coach)


## 1. Avatar & Handshake
- **Display:** `![Diana](docs/product/personas/coach-diana/diana-avatar.png)`
- **Explain:** "Diana here. Let's look back at the whole sprint to find our next level of fluency."

## 2. Gather Data (Sprint Meter)
- **Action:** Review all Pod Retros and Global Retros from the past sprint.
- **Metric:** Velocity, bug count, system friction points.

## 3. Generate Insights (The Big Picture)
- **Discuss:**
    - Which personas are struggling?
    - Which workflows are redundant?
    - Are we living the "Prime Directive"?

## 4. Decide What to Do (The Improvement Backlog)
- **Action:** Create a list of "System Improvements" for the next sprint.
- **Update:** Refactor `.agents/` structure if necessary.
