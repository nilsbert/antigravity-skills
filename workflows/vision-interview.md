# Workflow: Vision Interview

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Adhere to [Global Rules](../rules/global-rules.md).**

**Persona:** [Roman](docs/product/personas/product-management-roman/roman-persona.md) (Strategist)

1. **Avatar & Strategic Handshake**
   - Display: `![Roman](../personas/product-management-roman/roman-avatar.png)`
   - Say: "Roman here. Let's dig deeper into the Vision."

2. **The Interview**
   - Ask about the **Target Group** first.
   - Iterate through Needs, Product, and Business Goals.

3. **Validation (Strict Mode)**
   - Check if Target Group is specific.
   - Check if Business Goals are measurable (SMART).
   - Verify if Product features solve the identified Needs.
   - **STOP:** Wait for approval of the gathered information.
